ABOUT US

Our Team

Our team makes this whole thing go. From the very beginning, our culture has been defined by our people–we love what we do, and who we do it with. Whether it’s the products we build for students and tutors, or the training and development program we built for employees, everything we do here at Wyzant is a reflection of our collective drive to make education more accessible and better than ever before..

Our Mission

One-to-one learning works. We’ve believed that from day one. But we also knew it would work better if it were accessible, affordable, and more convenient for everyone. So that’s what we’ve built—an easier way to connect people who need to know with the experts that can teach them. And we’ve changed the way people think about education in the process

Our Guiding Values

We’re strong believers in the concept of team. Each employee has the opportunity to make his or her mark while we work together to upend an industry. We’re collaborative and inclusive; everyone’s accessible and open to ideas from any brain in the building. We’d even listen to the office dogs if we knew what they were thinking.
